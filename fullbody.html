Farmer’s Carry · Dumbbell

Focus Area

Forearms, Grip, Core

Execution

Walk with heavy dumbbells, posture tall, core engaged.

Deadlift · Barbell

Focus Area

Back, Glutes, Hamstrings

Execution

Lift barbell from ground with flat back, drive hips forward.

Bench Press · Barbell

Focus Area

Chest, Shoulders, Triceps

Execution

Lower bar to chest, press up fully without locking elbows.

Squat · Barbell

Focus Area

Quads, Glutes, Core

Execution

Keep back straight, descend until thighs parallel, push up through heels.

Overhead Press · Dumbbell

Focus Area

Shoulders, Triceps, Core

Execution

Press dumbbells overhead, avoid arching your back.

Pull-Up · Bodyweight

Focus Area

Back, Biceps, Core

Execution

Pull chest to bar, control descent. No swinging.

Ab Wheel Rollout · Core

Focus Area

Abs, Core Stability

Execution

Roll forward slowly, core tight, return without collapsing hips.

Importance of Diet

Building muscle and gaining healthy weight isn’t just about lifting heavier — it’s about fueling your body right. A calorie surplus rich in proteins, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates helps your muscles recover and grow stronger. Balanced nutrition ensures your workouts actually transform into visible gains, not just sweat and effort.
