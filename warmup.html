Jumping Jacks · Cardio

Focus Area

Full Body, Cardio

Execution

Jump with feet apart, arms overhead, back down to starting position.

High Knees · Cardio

Focus Area

Legs, Core

Execution

Run in place, driving knees up to waist height quickly.

Arm Circles · Shoulders

Focus Area

Shoulders, Upper Body

Execution

Circle arms forward and backward in controlled motion.

Leg Swings · Hips

Focus Area

Hips, Legs

Execution

Swing one leg forward and backward, keep torso stable.

Lunges · Legs

Focus Area

Legs, Glutes

Execution

Step forward, lower knee to 90°, push back to start.

Torso Twists · Core

Focus Area

Core, Obliques

Execution

Rotate torso side to side, keep hips stable.


Importance of Diet

Building muscle and gaining healthy weight isn’t just about lifting heavier — it’s about fueling your body right. A calorie surplus rich in proteins, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates helps your muscles recover and grow stronger. Balanced nutrition ensures your workouts actually transform into visible gains, not just sweat and effort.
