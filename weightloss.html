Jumping Jacks · Cardio

Jump feet wide and swing your arms overhead; return to start rhythmically. Perfect warm-up to ignite your heart rate.

Burpees · Full Body

Drop to a plank, jump back up, and leap. Each rep trains your endurance and torches calories fast.

Mountain Climbers · Core

Alternate your knees toward your chest in plank position — pure burn for your core and cardio system.

Jump Squat · Legs

Squat low and explode upward. Builds explosive power and helps in fat loss through intense muscle engagement.

Push-ups · Upper Body

Strengthen chest, shoulders, and arms. Boosts upper-body endurance and helps sculpt lean muscle.

Plank · Core

Hold your body straight like a board. Enhances stability and tightens the core muscles.

High Knees · Cardio

Run in place, driving knees high. A cardio blaster that wakes every muscle up.

Side Plank · Core

Balance on one forearm, stack your feet, and hold. Strengthens obliques and builds side stability.

Bicycle Crunches · Abs

Alternate elbow-to-knee motions for sharp ab definition and cardio synergy.

Skipping Rope · Cardio

Simple yet deadly effective. Jumping rope burns fat fast and elevates stamina like nothing else.

Importance of Diet in Weight Loss

Weight loss A balanced diet fuels your workouts and speeds up fat loss. Combine cardio with clean eating — high protein, low sugar, and enough hydration. Remember, you can’t out-train a bad diet.
