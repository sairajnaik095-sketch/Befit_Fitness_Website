Surya Namaskar · Warmup

Full-body warmup that enhances flexibility and energizes the body.

Tadasana · Posture

Improves posture and steadiness. A foundation pose for many asanas.

Trikonasana · Balance

Opens hips and shoulders, stretches hamstrings, and improves balance.

Bhujangasana · Spine

Strengthens the spine, stretches the chest, shoulders, and abdomen.

Adho Mukha Svanasana · Stretch

Relieves tension, strengthens arms and legs, and stretches the back.

Vajrasana · Digestion

Improves digestion and posture, and promotes calmness of mind.

Padmasana · Meditation

Enhances concentration, helps with meditation, and improves posture.

Dhanurasana · Back Strength

Strengthens the back and opens the chest, shoulders, and thighs.

Shavasana · Relaxation

Relaxes the body completely and restores energy after practice.

Anulom Vilom · Breathing

Balances breathing, improves focus, and relieves stress naturally.

Importance of Diet

Building muscle and gaining healthy weight isn’t just about lifting heavier — it’s about fueling your body right. A calorie surplus rich in proteins, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates helps your muscles recover and grow stronger. Balanced nutrition ensures your workouts actually transform into visible gains, not just sweat and effort.
